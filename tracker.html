Credit Tracker

Plan ahead.


As NHS members, you are responsible for making sure that you fulfill your credit requirements each semester and that your credit records are accurate. Below, you may view your current record of credits and probations, as well as how many more you will need by the end of the semester. If you notice any errors, please notify us via this Google Form.

In addition to your verified credits, you may add planned credits. These are for your personal benefit to help better organize your time and get a sense of where you are in terms of credits and whether you might be at risk; otherwise, you do not need to worry about them.

To the right, you will also find a tentative calendar for dates of NHS events of the 2018-2019 school year! Please plan your time accordingly, especially for mandatory events like Open House and Graduation.

We look forward to working with you!
