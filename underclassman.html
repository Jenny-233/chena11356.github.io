Underclassman Applications

"Plans are of little importance, but planning is essential."

Welcome to the NHS application portal for freshmen and sophomores! While only juniors and seniors are allowed to apply to NHS, you may work on your application ahead of time and upload verification materials as necessary. Then, when the time comes, you may transfer your information into a junior or senior application. To see updated credit requirements and inputs, please save your application with the button on the right.

This service is only for your convenience and will not directly impact the quality of your application nor your chances of acceptance. However, considering that you are planning so far ahead, it is likely that you have the stellar characteristics typical of an NHS member!

Please save your application regularly.

For an overview of the application process, FAQ, and the Credit Table, please click here. Please contact nhs@bxscience.edu with any concerns or questions regarding the application.

Signatures and Verification

To verify your credits, we will need you to upload this paper version of the application as well. Note that if you have been tracking credits since freshman or sophomore year, the underclassman paper application is acceptable as well. Your paper application should be identical to your electronic application, and signed by the appropriate faculty members. When you are ready to upload your paper application, follow the instructions below:

  1. Scan your file in pdf format and save as "your last name, your first name." For example, "DeSilva, Paul." Do not include the quotation marks.
  2. Please be sure that cookies are enabled in your browsers preferences.
  3. Upload to Dropbox here.
  4. Attach a single file.
  5. Fill in first name, last name, and email address.
  6. Click the Upload button.
  7. You will receive a confirmation email (provided that you supplied the correct email address) from Dropbox, indicating that your files have been uploaded successfully.

Out-of-School Activity Letters

Letters must be written on the letterhead of the organization for which you volunteered and signed by the adult that supervised your participation. When you are ready to upload your verification letter(s), follow the instructions below:

  1. Scan your file in pdf format and save as "your last name, your first name." For example, "DeSilva, Paul." Do not include the quotation marks.
  2. Please be sure that cookies are enabled in your browsers preferences.
  3. Upload to Dropbox here.
  4. Attach your verification letter. If you are submitting multiple letters, please attach them as a single file.
  5. Fill in first name, last name, and email address.
  6. Click the Upload button.
  7. You will receive a confirmation email (provided that you supplied the correct email address) from Dropbox, indicating that your files have been uploaded successfully.
