Privacy Policy

The Bronx Science NHS Web App is a service provided by the Bronx Science National Honor Society (NHS) at no cost and is intended for prospective applicants and current members to apply, track their credits, and acquire information regarding the Bronx Science NHS.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information through this web app.

If you choose to use the services on the web app which involve the input of personal information (i.e., if you sign in through Google), then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The personal information collected is used for streamlining the process of Bronx Science NHS applications and events, as well as providing and improving the service. Your information will not be shared with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Your Google account sign-in details will not be visible to anybody, as sign-in will be conducted through Google API's secure OAuth 2.0 sign-in protocol.

Information Collection and Use

If you choose to use this web app to sign in as a current or prospective NHS member, you will be required to provide certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to name, email, and year in high school. The information you provide will be retained on a Google Spreadsheet for purposes of documentation and consideration of applications and maintaining membership with the Bronx Science NHS.

The app does not use third party services that would collect information used to identify you.

Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory.

This service does not use these “cookies” explicitly. However, the app may use third party code and libraries that use “cookies”, such as Google Analytics, to collect information and improve services. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse these cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of this service.

Security

We value your trust in providing us your personal information, and thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it through backups and the appropriate security measures. Still, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure and reliable, and so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Links to Other Sites

This service may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by the Bronx Science NHS. Therefore, we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children’s Privacy

This service does not address anyone under the age of 13. No personally identifiable information will be knowingly collected from children under 13. In the case that a child under 13 has provided personal information through this web app, it will be immediately deleted from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can perform the necessary actions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Assuming you have provided your email, you will be notified of any changes to this Privacy Policy. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the Bronx Science NHS Board at nhs@bxscience.edu.
